Support

Help with MaxSend on Mac and iPad

Need help? Check the FAQ below. For billing and downloads, use Apple Support. For MaxSend-specific questions, email us.

Contact

Email: support@codewithik.com

What do I need to start sending?

An SMTP account from your email provider or IT team (host, port, username, password), plus a recipient list and message content. Use Test Connection in SMTP Accounts before launching a campaign.

Does MaxSend guarantee inbox delivery?

No. MaxSend sends well-formed mail through your server, but inbox placement depends on SPF/DKIM/DMARC, domain reputation, list quality, and recipient server policies. See the in-app Deliverability guide.

Where is my data stored?

Campaigns and recipients are stored locally on your Mac or iPad in SQLite. SMTP passwords are in the system Keychain. Data is not uploaded to a MaxSend cloud service.

Why do I see "503 sender already given" or RCPT rejections?

503 errors often mean the SMTP session needed a reset after a failed recipient (fixed in recent versions). 550 rejections usually mean the receiving server rejected your From address or recipient—check that your From mailbox exists and your domain has proper authentication.

Can I pause and resume a large send?

Yes. Sending state is persisted in the local database. You can pause, quit the app, and resume later from the Send tab.

Is MaxSend available on iPhone?

MaxSend is built for iPad and Mac. iPhone is not currently supported.
