How it works

Four steps to your first send

Connect your server, build a campaign, import recipients, and launch from the Send dashboard.

Add SMTP

Enter host, port, security mode, and credentials. Verify with Test Connection.

Compose

Write subject and body with merge tokens like {{firstName}} and attach files.

Import recipients

Map CSV columns or paste addresses. Invalid emails are filtered; duplicates are skipped.

Send

Set throughput and workers, watch live progress, and pause or stop anytime.

Responsible use

Built for permission-based mail

Use MaxSend only for mail your recipients expect. Comply with CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL, and local law. MaxSend does not bypass spam filters.

Inbox placement is not guaranteed. Authentication, reputation, and content determine whether mail lands in the inbox.
