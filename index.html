Features

Professional sending, on your terms

Everything a high-volume mail client should handle—composition, lists, pacing, and protocol—without renting someone else's infrastructure.

Massive recipient lists

Stream CSV imports with millions of rows. Paste leads, map columns, validate addresses, dedupe per campaign, and browse a searchable paginated list—all stored locally in SQLite.

Rich HTML compose

Plain text and HTML with live preview, formatting toolbar, merge fields, attachments, and Cc/Bcc.

Keychain-secured SMTP

SSL/TLS and STARTTLS. Passwords never touch the database. Test connection before you send.

Throttled delivery

Parallel connections, rate limits, retries, live ETA, and copyable error logs.

Deliverability guide

In-app tips on SPF, DKIM, DMARC, warmup, and permission-based sending.

How it works

Four steps to your first campaign

From SMTP setup to live send dashboard—designed for long runs you can pause and resume.

01

Connect SMTP

Add host, port, security, and credentials. Verify with Test Connection.

02

Compose

Write subject and body with tokens like {{firstName}}. Attach files.

03

Import

CSV column mapping or paste-in. Invalid emails filtered; duplicates skipped.

04

Send

Configure workers and throughput. Watch progress. Pause anytime.

Responsible use

Built for permission-based mail

Use MaxSend only when recipients expect to hear from you. Comply with CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL, and local law. MaxSend does not bypass spam filters.
